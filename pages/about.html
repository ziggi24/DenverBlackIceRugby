Overview & Mission

Denver Black Ice Women's Rugby Football Club (WRFC) is a Division 1 and Division 2 women's rugby team in the Rocky Mountain Rugby Football Union. Established in 1991, Black Ice is the longest standing women's rugby club in Colorado.

We are proud to have competed in Division 1 rugby for over 30 seasons and are regarded as an integral part of the Denver rugby community. For more than 30 years, Black Ice has boasted competitive players from across the country, in addition to having a strong side for beginners and rookies to rugby.

Mission

Black Ice WRFC strives to maintain a team bonded by respect and love for the game of rugby. We aim to create an inclusive environment committed to achieving our best as both individuals and as a team. Through this relentless dedication and passion, we will build a community of rugby in Denver and set a precedent for women's teams to motivate and support each other in the pursuit of athletic achievement.

In recent years, rugby has become one of the fastest growing sports in the United States, with women contributing the largest growing segment. Black Ice WRFC is committed to advancing the sport by creating a competitive environment focused on athletic development.

Approximately forty women make up the core of Black Ice Rugby - ranging from 18 to 40 years old. This diverse group draws in networks ranging from competitive athletes to local businesses to active community members. Black Ice WRFC prides itself on engaging and giving back to the community that has supported its growth for the past 30 years.

Our Commitment

  • Inclusive, community-first culture welcoming all experience levels
  • Competitive excellence at both Division 1 and Division 2 levels
  • Player development and leadership opportunities
  • Maintaining our legacy as Colorado's premier women's rugby club

Players of all experience levels are welcome and encouraged to take part in the inclusive, fun, and competitive culture of Black Ice Women's Rugby. Interested in joining? Contact us for practice details.
